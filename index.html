About

The Mathematics Entrepreneurship Engineering and Computer Science Organization (MEECSO) is a school club for students who want to gain invaluable experience and expertise on several key topics that they cannot receive elsewhere. MEECSO is a club where likeminded students expand upon the topics that they learn in class and apply them in the real world.

Projects

Students will work on different projects surrounding issues that they see in their community using the skills that they may pick up on their own or in the classroom. Students will improve their craft whilst helping out their local community.

A project could be as simple as forming an afterschool tutoring program or as complex as building a new app that will revolutionarize the world.

Projects

  • Web Design and Development
  • HTML5 Game Development
  • Engineering Experimentation
